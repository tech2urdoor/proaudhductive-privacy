# ProAuDHDuctive — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 22 May 2026

ProAuDHDuctive is a productivity app for AuDHD (autism + ADHD) brains. It is designed to be local-first: your tasks, routines, brain dumps, and chat history live on your device by default. This policy describes what that means in practice, and what changes if you choose to connect an external AI provider.

If anything here is unclear, email **tech2urdoor@gmail.com**.

---

## 1. What we collect from you

**Nothing.** ProAuDHDuctive has no servers of its own and runs no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or identifiers. We do not create an account for you, and we do not see your data.

## 2. What is stored on your device

The app stores the following on your iPhone or iPad, inside the app's own protected storage (iOS AsyncStorage / Android equivalent). None of it leaves the device unless you take an action that sends it elsewhere (see §3 and §8).

- Your tasks (titles, optional notes, energy level, time-of-day, subtasks, status)
- Your routines and recurrence rules
- Your brain dump entries (text, capture timestamp, source)
- Your chat history with the in-app AI assistant
- Your settings (theme, reduce-motion, notification time, AI provider configuration including any API key you enter)

Uninstalling the app deletes all of this. There is no cloud backup; nothing syncs across devices.

## 3. AI assistant — local or your choice of cloud provider

The app has four optional AI surfaces:

- **Task suggestions** (the ✨ Suggest pill on the add-task screen)
- **Task breakdown** (Break into steps on the task detail screen)
- **Brain dump extraction** (turns a brain dump into actionable tasks)
- **Chat assistant** (the Ask screen)

All four work out of the box using built-in templates — **no AI is required**. If you want a real model, you pick a provider in Settings → AI assistant:

### Local

A model you run yourself on your own network or device (Ollama, llama.cpp, LM Studio, etc.). You enter the endpoint URL. **No data leaves your network.** We do not see it; nobody else does either.

### Cloud providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI-compatible)

If you choose a cloud provider and supply your own API key, the app sends data **directly from your device to that provider** using your key. The key never travels through any server we operate — we do not have any server. We do not see, log, or store any of the content that you send.

What is sent depends on which surface is in use:

| Surface | Data sent to your provider |
|---|---|
| Task suggestions | The partial title you typed, the titles of today's tasks, current time of day, and (if set) your selected energy level |
| Task breakdown | The task title and any notes on the task |
| Brain dump extraction | The raw brain dump text |
| Chat assistant | Your chat history with the assistant, today's task titles + statuses + energy + start time, tomorrow's task titles + energy, and your most recent brain dumps (truncated, last 10 entries or last 7 days, whichever is smaller) |

Each cloud surface has an **individual on/off toggle** in Settings under "Send to {provider}". Turning a surface off keeps it on the built-in templates and prevents the app from sending anything to your provider for that surface, even while other surfaces remain enabled.

**Once data reaches your provider, their own privacy policy governs how it is used and retained.** We have no insight into or control over that. Their relevant policies as of this writing:

- OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- Google (Gemini): https://policies.google.com/privacy

Switching providers in Settings does not transfer any data — it just changes where future requests go.

## 4. Microphone and voice transcription

The brain dump screen has an optional voice capture button. When you hold it down:

- Your microphone is recorded only while you are holding the button
- The audio is transcribed **entirely on your device** using Apple's on-device Speech framework
- The audio is **not stored anywhere** — only the transcribed text is saved (as a brain dump entry)
- No audio data leaves your device

You can deny microphone permission and the rest of the app still works.

## 5. Notifications

If you enable Morning Summary in Settings, the app schedules a single daily **local** notification — meaning it is generated by your own device, on your device, with no server involved. You can disable it at any time. The app does not use remote push notifications.

## 6. No tracking, no advertising, no profiling

The app contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics tools, no third-party tracking pixels, no identifier-for-advertisers usage. We do not build a profile of you. We do not sell anything, because we do not have your data to sell.

## 7. Children

ProAuDHDuctive is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from anyone (see §1). If a child uses the app with their parent's consent, the same on-device-only model applies.

## 8. Reporting AI responses

If an AI response in the app feels wrong, harmful, or inaccurate, every assistant message can be reported via long-press → Report. Settings → Report an AI response opens the same flow for non-chat surfaces. The Report action opens your mail client with the response content prefilled so you can send it to **tech2urdoor@gmail.com**. No data is sent unless you tap Send in your mail client.

This is the channel for the App Store guideline 1.2 obligation to provide a way to report objectionable AI output.

## 9. Changes to this policy

If we change anything material — for example, adding a new AI provider, a new optional integration, or a new data category — we will update this page and bump the effective date at the top. Because the app stores no email address or account, there is no mailing-list notification. Re-checking this URL is the way to see updates.
